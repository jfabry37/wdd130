Centerville, Washington

Centerville, Washington is a small unincorporated community located in Klickitat County in the south-central part of the state. Nestled in a rural and scenic area near the Columbia River Gorge, it offers wide open spaces, rolling hills, and a peaceful, agricultural setting. With a population of just a few hundred residents, Centerville is known for its tight-knit community, farming roots, and proximity to outdoor recreation, including hiking, fishing, and windsurfing. Its quiet charm and natural beauty make it a serene spot away from the bustle of city life.
